Privacy Policy for Success360

Introduction

Success360 ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Success360 (the "App").

Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number
  • Profile Data: Age, gender, fitness goals, career objectives, business information
  • Content: Photos, videos, workout logs, career documents, business plans
  • Communication: Messages, feedback, support requests

Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: App features used, time spent, interaction patterns
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Analytics: Performance metrics, crash reports, error logs

Health and Fitness Data

  • Body Measurements: Weight, height, body composition
  • Progress Photos: Before/after transformation images
  • Workout Data: Exercise routines, performance metrics, video recordings
  • Wellness Information: Sleep patterns, nutrition logs, health goals

How We Use Your Information

Core App Functionality

  • Provide personalized fitness recommendations and coaching
  • Deliver career advancement tools and business growth strategies
  • Track progress and generate insights
  • Sync data across your devices

AI-Powered Features

  • Generate personalized workout plans using artificial intelligence
  • Provide AI-driven career guidance and resume optimization
  • Offer intelligent business coaching and strategy recommendations
  • Analyze progress photos for transformation tracking

Communication and Support

  • Send important app updates and notifications
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Share relevant tips and educational content

Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve app functionality
  • Develop new features based on user needs
  • Ensure app security and prevent fraud

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We Do Not Sell Your Personal Information

Success360 does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

Limited Sharing Scenarios

  • Service Providers: Third-party services that help us operate the app (cloud storage, analytics, payment processing)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or legal process
  • Safety and Security: To protect the rights, property, or safety of Success360, users, or others
  • Business Transfers: In connection with mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales

AI and Machine Learning

  • Your data may be used in aggregate and anonymized form to improve our AI algorithms
  • Individual data is processed locally on your device when possible
  • We use industry-standard encryption for data transmission and storage

Data Security

Security Measures

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data transmission
  • Secure cloud storage with enterprise-grade protection
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Multi-factor authentication for account access

Data Retention

  • Personal information is retained while your account is active
  • You can delete your account and data at any time
  • Some information may be retained for legal compliance or legitimate business purposes

Your Privacy Rights

Access and Control

  • View Your Data: Access all personal information we have about you
  • Update Information: Modify or correct your profile and account data
  • Delete Data: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Data Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format

Communication Preferences

  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications
  • Notification Settings: Control push notifications and alerts
  • Privacy Settings: Adjust data sharing and visibility preferences

Children's Privacy

Success360 is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

International Data Transfers

If you access Success360 from outside the United States, your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States where our servers are located and our central database is operated.

Third-Party Integrations

Fitness Trackers and Wearables

  • Integration with Apple Health, Google Fit, and other fitness platforms
  • Data sharing is optional and controlled by your device settings
  • We only access data necessary for app functionality

Social Media

  • Optional social sharing features
  • You control what information is shared publicly
  • We do not access your social media data without explicit permission

Payment Processing

  • Subscription payments processed through Apple App Store and Google Play Store
  • We do not store your payment card information
  • Payment data is handled according to platform privacy policies

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes through:

  • In-app notifications
  • Email notifications to your registered address
  • Updates posted in the app

Regional Privacy Rights

California Residents (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act, including the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to delete personal information, and the right to opt-out of the sale of personal information.

European Union Residents (GDPR)

EU residents have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation, including the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, and data portability.

Consent

By using Success360, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and agree to the collection, use, and disclosure of your information as described herein.
